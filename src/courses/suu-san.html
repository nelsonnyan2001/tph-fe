Syllabus - What you will learn from this course

LEVEL

1

23 hours to complete

Basic Coding for Kids 1

This course is an introductory computer science and programming basic course. Students will develop their understanding of the basic concepts such as sequencing, programming, loops, and events with helpful videos and puzzles using Code.org.

LEVEL

2

21 hours to complete

Basic Coding for Kids 2

This course will further develop students’ ideas of block programming and digital literacy. By the end of the course, students will know problem-solving methods and be able to create interactive projects that they can share.

LEVEL

3

17 hours to complete

Hello AI

In this course, the concepts of AI such as AI Fundamentals, Supervised Learning, Unsupervised Learning, Reinforcement Learning, Understanding and Generating Language, and AI Ethics are introduced. Students will be able to

  1. 1. Understand the basic concepts of Artificial Intelligence
  2. 2. Know the applications of AI
  3. 3. Explain AI to other people
